## Executive Summary

The jamesajacobs.net React demo site serves **10 distinct audience segments** with conflicting needs. Current friction points include:
- **High-value audiences** (attorneys, utilities) lack clear pathways
- **Technical depth** alienates non-technical property owners
- **Academic citations** reduce readability for 80% of visitors
- **Missing CTAs** at decision points

**Key Finding:** Optimizing for the top 3 audiences (Attorneys, Utilities, Property Owners) will capture 70-80% of conversion opportunities.

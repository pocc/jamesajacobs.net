# Johnston Family Tree

Complete genealogy of descendants of John Taylor Johnston (1820-1893) and Frances Colles Johnston.

## Overview

This family tree represents the Union of Relative Strangers - descendants of John Taylor Johnston, who was:
- First President of the Metropolitan Museum of Art (1870-1889)
- President of the Central Railroad of New Jersey
- Prominent art collector and philanthropist

## Primary Branches

The family tree divides into six primary branches through the children of John Taylor Johnston and Frances Colles:

1. **Emily Johnston** → married Henry Lockwood deForest
2. **Colles Johnston** → married Eva Johnston (later Coe)
3. **John Herbert Johnston** → two children
4. **Eva Johnston** → married Henry Julian Mali
5. **Frances Johnston** → married Pierre Mali
6. **Harriet Johnston** → married Robert Bowdoin (not in current data)
